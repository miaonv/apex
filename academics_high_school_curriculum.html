APEX Global Academy High School Courses

Once a student enrolls at APEX Global Academy, they are assigned a Home Advisor who assists the student to plan a course of study. Sample 2 and 4 year plans are in the Appendix. Home Advisors monitor student academic progress, observe them in classes, assist them to meet with academic success, and communicate directly with all parties regarding student or parent concerns. College counselors help students select appropriate colleges and complete applications.
