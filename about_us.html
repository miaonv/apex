About APEX Global Academy

Excel in High School and Beyond from the Comfort of Home!

APEX Global Academy, a non-profit organization, offers international students the chance to take both online and in-person high school and University-level classes. Through strategic partnerships with top American schools and Universities, we provide a range of non-credit and for-credit courses taught by top educators, meticulously selected by APEX Global Academy.

We offer individualized teaching that nurtures each student’s personality and unique talents in a relaxed and enjoyable environment, embodying what education should be.

As a 100% American-owned institution, we operate with complete transparency and adhere to the highest ethical standards. Our dedicated teams support students and families throughout the educational journey, and we are committed to giving back to the community through charitable donations and volunteer activities.

Our Approach

We are committed to preparing each student for top American Universities through personalized educational experiences. Our one-to-one and small class sizes guarantee individualized attention and instruction tailored to each student’s unique learning style, talents, and interests. At APEX, personalized learning means teachers adapt their instruction to meet each student’s unique needs. This approach not only customizes the curriculum to align with individual interests but also creates new learning opportunities that drive academic and personal success. Our pursuit of excellence drives us to continuously expand our course offerings and maintain a curriculum that exceeds the highest academic standards, including Common Core Standards and ACT Benchmarks. APEX is dedicated to pioneering the best methods and practices for online learning, ensuring that every student receives an unparalleled educational experience.

Our Beliefs

Collaborative Framework: We believe in combining our efforts to create a supportive framework where students evolve into confident, knowledgeable, and responsible thinkers of the future.

Adaptability: We equip students with the skills necessary to adapt to an ever-changing world.

Ethical Decision-Making: We emphasize the importance of developing the ability to use ethics as a guide in making decisions, which we consider the hallmark of education.

Creativity and Passion: We value each student's creativity, imagination, and passion.

Global Citizenship: We guide students to become active, informed global citizens.

Holistic Development: We help students reach their full potential, not only academically but also emotionally and socially.

Innovative Education: We integrate innovative teaching methods, incorporate technology, and promote critical thinking skills.

Inclusive Environment: We embrace diverse perspectives and foster an inclusive environment.

Our Services
  • Summer Online Bridge Course: Designed to prepare incoming new students for the challenges of US private high schools.
  • Semester Online Courses: Offering both for-credit and non-credit courses to support students' academic progress during the school year.
  • Academic Tutoring: Personalized tutoring sessions with top university students or experienced teachers to help students excel in their studies.
  • Gap Year Online Courses: Tailored courses to help students enhance their competitiveness for college admissions during their gap year.
  • College Counseling: Comprehensive guidance to facilitate a smooth transition to top US universities.
