Admissions Process

Unlock a world of learning

Step1Application
Prospective students should complete the APEX Global Academy Application and submit official transcripts and/or test scores.
Apply Now
Step2 Evaluation reserve
Prospective students will interview with an APEX staff member to assess the student's readiness for the program.
Step3 Direction
Students accepted into APEX will be assigned an Admissions Coordinator to provide guidance through the enrollment proces s. The Admissions Coordinator will speak with the student and their family to understand the student’s needs and present an overview of APEX's course offerings.
Step4 Academic Goals
The Admissions Coordinator identifies courses and/or programs that potentially meet the student’s academic goals.
Step5 Home Advisor
Upon deciding to enroll in APEX, students are assigned a Home Advisor who will assist the student throughout their time at APEX,. Junior and Senior students will also be assigned a College Counselor who will assist them in preparing for university study.
Step6 Plan of Study
With the assistance of the Home Advisor, the student will create a Plan of Study and begin enrolling in courses. Once the tuition payment has been received, a welcome letter is sent to the family, teachers are assigned to the student, and the student is granted access to the learning management system. The newly enrolled student may begin coursework immediately.
